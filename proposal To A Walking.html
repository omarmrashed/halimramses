A proposal To A Walking Route in Emad Eddine street

A booklet
2024

Through the booklet, Halim proposes a walking route in Emad Eddine Street in Cairo that includes performing several exercises that test the possible opportunities of physical and sensory interactions with Emad Eddine Street. The exercises avoid the use of imagination and prefer to rely on actions such as identification, physical presence, and storytelling to search for the current possible opportunities for leisure.

The project was developed as part of Medina Archive Programme,
curated by: Ahmed Shawky Hassan. Participants: Melanie Partamian, Halim Ramses, Nourhan Maayouf, Mohamed Abo Gabal and Aya Tariq.

The photo on the homepage: Nourhan Maayouf.
